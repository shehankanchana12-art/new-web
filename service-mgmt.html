Key Features

Complete Job Tracking for Service Businesses

From the moment a customer walks in with a broken device to the day it's collected — every step is tracked, recorded, and reportable.

📝
Job Card Management
Create detailed job cards for every service request. Record fault description, estimated cost, and expected delivery date.
👷
Technician Assignment
Assign jobs to specific technicians. Track individual workload, completion rates, and performance metrics.
📱
Customer SMS Alerts
Automatic SMS to customers when job is received, in progress, and ready for collection. No more phone calls needed.
🔩
Spare Parts Inventory
Track spare parts stock. Deduct parts used per job automatically. Get low-stock alerts and reorder reminders.
🛡
Warranty Tracking
Record warranty periods for repairs. Automatically flag warranty claims and track post-repair service history.
💰
Billing & Estimates
Generate quotations, advance receipts, and final invoices. Track payments and outstanding balances per job.
📊
Service Reports
Revenue per technician, job completion rates, pending jobs, parts usage, and monthly service analytics.
📷
Before/After Photos
Attach photos to job cards to document device condition on intake and completion. Protect against disputes.
