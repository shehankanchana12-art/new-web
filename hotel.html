Core Modules

Everything a Modern Hotel Needs

Our Hotel Management System covers the entire guest lifecycle from booking to checkout, with integrated restaurant and housekeeping modules.

📅
Reservation Management
Online and walk-in bookings with room availability calendar. Instant confirmation and room assignment.
🛎
Front Desk Operations
Fast check-in and check-out. Guest profile management with stay history and preferences stored securely.
🛏
Room & Housekeeping
Room status dashboard (clean, occupied, dirty, maintenance). Assign housekeeping tasks and track completion.
🍽
Restaurant Integration
In-room dining and restaurant bills posted directly to guest room accounts. Unified checkout billing.

Billing & Folio
Auto-generated guest folio with room charges, extras, taxes. Accept deposits, partial payments, and corporate billing.

Occupancy Analytics
Daily occupancy rates, revenue per room (RevPAR), booking sources, and seasonal trend reports.
🔑
Multi-Room Type Support
Configure single, double, suite, AC/non-AC, and seasonal rate plans. Group booking support included.
📱
Guest Communication
Automated SMS/WhatsApp booking confirmations, reminders, and checkout summaries to guests.
