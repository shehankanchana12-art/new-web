Key Features

Run Your Restaurant Like a Pro

Our system handles every aspect of restaurant operations — from the front of house to the kitchen — with one integrated platform.

🪑
Table & Floor Management
Visual floor plan layout. See table status (available, occupied, reserved) at a glance. Merge and split tables easily.
📋
Digital Menu & Ordering
Tablet or desktop ordering with categorized menu. Add modifiers, special instructions, and combos for each item.
🖨
Kitchen Order Tickets (KOT)
Orders sent instantly to kitchen printers. Separate printers for grill, bar, and bakery sections. No lost orders.
🧾
Fast Billing & Settlements
One-click bill generation with item summary, taxes, and service charges. Split bills between guests with ease.
📦
Ingredient Stock Tracking
Link menu items to ingredients. Auto-deduct stock with every order. Get alerts when items run low.
🏃
Delivery & Takeaway Mode
Separate queues for dine-in, takeaway, and delivery orders. Customer tracking and estimated ready times.
📊
Daily Sales Reports
End-of-day reports with revenue, top dishes, peak hours, table turnover, and waiter-wise sales breakdown.
🎁
Discounts & Happy Hour
Schedule automatic discounts, happy hour pricing, and loyalty points for returning customers.
