Core Features

Everything Your Business Needs at the Counter

Our POS systems are designed for simplicity, speed, and accuracy — built to handle busy retail environments without technical complexity.

📦
Real-Time Inventory
Track stock levels automatically with every sale. Get low-stock alerts and manage multiple item categories with ease.
🔖
Barcode & QR Scanning
Full barcode scanner support for fast item lookup, plus QR-code label printing for your own products.
🧾
Digital & Print Receipts
Thermal printer support for instant receipts. Also send digital receipts via SMS or WhatsApp to customers.

Customer Loyalty
Built-in loyalty point system and customer account management to reward repeat shoppers and boost retention.

Sales Reports & Analytics
Daily, weekly and monthly sales summaries. Profit reports, top-selling items, and shift-wise breakdowns.

Multi-User Access
Assign cashiers, managers and admin roles with different permissions. Audit trails for every transaction.

Multiple Payment Modes
Cash, card, credit, and mobile payments. Split billing and credit sales with customer account tracking.

Cloud Backup
All data securely backed up to the cloud in real-time. Access your business data from anywhere, anytime.
