Core Features

Everything a Tailor Shop Needs

Say goodbye to paper order books and missed deliveries. Our tailoring system keeps every order, measurement, and customer perfectly organized.

📏
Customer Measurements
Store complete measurement profiles per customer. Access any time for repeat orders — no re-measuring needed.
🗂
Order Management
Create and track every order with garment type, fabric, design notes, and special instructions. View all pending orders at a glance.
📅
Delivery Scheduling
Set promised delivery dates. Get automatic alerts for upcoming and overdue deliveries. Never miss a promise to a customer again.
📱
Customer SMS Alerts
Send automatic SMS when order is confirmed, ready for trial, or ready for collection. Customers stay informed.
🧵
Fabric & Material Tracking
Record fabric details, quantity used per order, and remaining stock. Link materials to orders for accurate cost calculation.
🧾
Order Receipts & Billing
Print or send digital receipts on order intake. Generate final invoices with advance and balance tracking per customer.
👗
Garment Categories
Support for sarees, shirts, trousers, kurtas, suits, school uniforms, wedding wear, and custom categories you define.
📊
Business Reports
Orders received vs completed, revenue reports, fabric usage, busiest periods, and tailor-wise productivity tracking.
